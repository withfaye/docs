Use the Faye Partner Sales API to quote travel protection, present plan documents, collect payment, and issue policies for travelers.

New integrations should use the V2 quote and purchase flow:

1. Create a quote with trip, residency, traveler, product, and add-on details.
2. Show the traveler the quote price and sample policy document.
3. Tokenize the traveler's credit card with your approved payment provider.
4. Purchase the quote with the quote hash and payment token.
5. Store the returned policy code, policy URL, and receipt URL.

## Base URLs

| Environment | Base URL |
| --- | --- |
| Production | `https://partnerapi.withfaye.com` |
| Staging | `https://partnerapi.fayehq.com` |
| Development | `https://partnerapi.faye-dev.com` |

Use staging credentials against staging URLs and production credentials against production URLs. Tokens are environment-specific.

## Core objects

| Object | What it represents |
| --- | --- |
| Quote | A priced offer for one product. A quote includes `currentPrice`, `quoteHash`, add-on availability, `policyExampleUrl`, `quoteUrl`, and `quotePageUrl` when traveler details are present. |
| Multi-quote | A group of quotes for multiple products. It includes a `groupHash` and one quote item per requested product. |
| Policy | The purchased coverage. A successful purchase returns `purchaseId`, one or more traveler policy IDs, `totalPrice`, and policy or receipt URLs when available. |
| Advisor | A licensed advisor profile under your partner account. Pass `advisorId` on quote and purchase calls when your integration is advisor-led. |
